Create an Event

Add the event name, start time, and duration. Only one person needs to create the Event, everyone else just needs to scan the KrewCode with the icon!

FAQ

How does Krewdar work without cellular service or internet?

Krewdar takes advantage of Bluetooth Low Energy, allowing friends to see when they're close to each other by broadcasting and scanning for small packets of data. This data is just an arbitrary identifier that is generated when one person creates an Event.

What does the distance text on the Nearby tab mean?

"Immediate" is displayed when your friend is really close by, followed by "Near" then "Far". You might see "???" when your friend is extremely far away, or when the Bluetooth signal is weak.

Why can't I set my own messages?

Keeping the data as small as possible means more reliable detection of the Bluetooth signals, which means you'll be able to find you're friends more often. With that in mind, we plan on expanding the pre-set messages available.
